About

Welcome to the Caltech Critters online pet store! Here you can find animals found at the California Institute of Technology for adoption. Caltech is situated in Southern California, so these critters will do best in warm and dry climates! Make sure to check out the animals available one the animals tab. If you'd like to adopt, press the adopt button and proceed to the adoption confirmation page from the cart.
